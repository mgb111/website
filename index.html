VR Walkthrough: Aquaculture in India in VR 360 | MPEDA

A VR walkthrough experience for India's shrimp farming sector. Created for the Marine Products Export Development Authority (MPEDA), this immersive demo offers a ground-level perspective of aquaculture operations.
