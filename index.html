What is AR?

Augmented Reality overlays digital content onto the real world through your camera, adding interactive 3D visuals, info and effects to what you see.

  • E‑commerce: virtual try‑ons, product previews
  • Packaging: scan to unlock stories, tutorials, rewards
  • Events: interactive photo‑ops and brand games
  • Education: 3D visualizations and guided learning
  • Real estate and automotive: in‑place visualization

What is VR?

Virtual Reality immerses users inside a fully 3D environment, enabling presence, focus and interaction beyond a flat screen.

  • Events and expos: captivating product storytelling
  • Sales enablement: guided interactive demos
  • Training: safe simulation of real‑world scenarios
  • Tourism and real estate: virtual tours
  • Education: immersive labs and explorations
